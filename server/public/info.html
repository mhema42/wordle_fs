Information page

In the navigation bar, you can choose WORDLE to play the game, go to this INFORMATION page, or list the HIGHSCORE of players who has entered their name.

To play the game, you have the option to select if you want only unique letters or if there can be two or more of the same letter in the word to guess for. Then you choose length on word to guess for (4, 5 or 6 letters).

After you made your choise, you enter a word you guess is correct and the game shows if a letter in the guessed word is correct (green color), correct but missplaced (yellow color) and incorrect (red color), you keep on guessing until you guessed the correct word.

When you guessed the correct word, the game will display how many guesses you made and your time. And you have the option to enter your name to send result to highscore-list.
